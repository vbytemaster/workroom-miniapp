How to generate images?
Type a description in the AI chat. Specify ratio, style, quality in the prompt. E.g. "a cat in space, 16:9"
How to create videos?
Switch to WAN 2.2 model and describe your scene. Or say "make a video from this" after generating an image.
How do credits work?

Can AI remember context?
Yes. Say "make it bluer" or "now as video" — AI keeps context within the session.
